Here is a basic, chronological timeline of the plot, tracking the events from the backstory to the current narrative timeline:
The Backstory
● February 3, 1995: Marie’s teenage brother, Paul, crashes his car and dies. The other driver, a pregnant Jane Doe, dies, but her baby is saved via an emergency C-section. Marie's politically ambitious father covers up the accident.
● Late February 1995: Marie (8 years old) survives the crash and begins experiencing a terrifying, recurring nightmare of a dark basement, a trapped woman, a lunging man, and a gunshot. The saved baby is quietly placed into the state foster care system.
Act I: The Return of the Dream
● Present Day (30 Years Later): Marie moves into her late grandparents' Garden District home following her divorce from Simon LeBlanc. The nightmare returns instantly.
● Days 1–3: Marie meets her glamorous across-the-street neighbor, Alex Miller, and experiences intense déjà vu. She also meets an IPS delivery man, Vincent Trahan, who perfectly matches the physical profile of the killer in her dreams.
● The First Hit: The killer abducts a blond, blue-eyed man matching a specific psychological profile, brutally murders him, and dumps the body on a Riverbend levee.
● The Investigation Begins: Terrified the dream is a premonition, Marie consults a bayou voodoo spiritualist, Lois Bergeron. Marie meets Lois’s protective security expert brother, Leo, and a romance sparks.
Act II: Surveillance & Escalation
● The Stalking Phase: Marie finds Vincent's address on Fern Street and begins running late-night stakeouts. Leo discovers her files and furiously demands she stop for her own safety.
● The Second Hit: The killer abducts a second victim, Alcide Marchand, outside Jérôme’s Jazz Joint and later disposes of his remains in the Atchafalaya Basin.
● The Cemetery Fiasco: Marie tracks Vincent to Cat-o-Comb club and follows him into Lafayette Cemetery No. 1, believing he is about to murder a woman. Instead, she catches him in a consensual encounter, faces intense humiliation, and flees.
Act III: Broken Illusions & Bloodshed
● The Reality Check: Marie breaks her promise to Leo and confronts Alex to warn her about the dream. Alex shatters Marie's theory by stating she has no basement. Marie has a mental breakdown; her therapist diagnoses an acute psychotic break and prescribes medication.
● The Alliance: Alex visits Marie, validates her "suburban spy" tactics, and the two women team up to run a Saturday night stakeout on Vincent together.
● The Turning Point: Marie tracks down the 1995 crash nurse, Doris Bourgeois, learning the name of the resident doctor who delivered the baby (Dr. Thompson, now in Colombia).
